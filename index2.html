Andrey Kolchanov

I am a second year graduate student at Skolkovo Institute of Science and Technology (Skoltech). I am Special Student at MIT.

I am also a member of the MIT wireless Center.

Before this, I got my B.S in Computer Science degree from Microsoft Special Pilot CS Class(Yao Class), Institute for Theoretical Computer Science, Tsinghua University in 2011.
